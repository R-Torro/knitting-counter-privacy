Privacy Policy

Last updated: 26 May 2026

Knitting Counter ("the app") is a personal project tool for tracking knitting and crochet projects. This policy explains how your data is handled.

Data collection

The app does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All information you enter — project names, counter values, measurements, notes, and settings — is stored locally on your device only.

Third-party services

The app does not use any analytics, advertising, or third-party SDKs that collect data.

Data storage

Your data is stored in a local database on your device. It is not backed up to any server and is not accessible to anyone other than you.

Data deletion

Uninstalling the app removes all data stored by the app from your device.

Children

The app does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.
